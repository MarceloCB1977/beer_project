ABOUT

We realized that many people never tried Kraft Beer, mostly due to not knowing the styles, what to expect from those styles, and due to the elevated costs.

So we started to research all the beer Styles and their respective features, such as ABV(Alcohol By Volume), IBU(International Bitterness Units), SRM(Standard Reference Method), Harmonization, History and etc.

With that we created this website where the user inputs age, gender and the desired ABV(alcohol content), the IBU(bitternes), SRM(clarity) and gets a beer recommendation based on their input.

The goal here is in the near future to create a Reccomendation Model with Machine Learning, to reccomend beer Styles by user profile and location, recommending local breweries.

Expading the market and helping to spread the word from the smallest to the biggest brewers in the world. It's a ever growing market and this innovation it's extremely scalable, prone to great partnerships, sponsorships and evolution.

We are still adding information and details to our Database, so every information is appreciated.
